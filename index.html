Re-Gassing

from $159

The existing refrigerant is fully recovered in accordance with environmental regulations, followed by a system pressure and leak test. The air-conditioning system is then recharged with the manufacturer-specified refrigerant type and quantity. This process restores correct operating pressures, maximises cooling efficiency, ensures proper compressor lubrication, and supports optimal engine fuel economy.

A/C System Repairs

Diagnose from $120

Comprehensive diagnosis and repair of automotive air-conditioning systems, including electronic fault scanning, pressure analysis, and leak detection. Repairs may involve replacement of components such as compressors, condensers, evaporators, expansion valves, receiver-driers, seals, and hoses. All work is completed to manufacturer specifications to restore correct system pressures, cooling performance, reliability, and efficiency.
